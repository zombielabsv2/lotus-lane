Daisaku Ikeda on Youth

Ikeda Sensei's passionate encouragement to young people — the torchbearers of the future, the protagonists of a new era of peace.

15 quotes

“Youth is not a time of life — it is a state of mind. As long as you have a dream, as long as you have a fighting spirit, you are forever young.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“Nothing is more precious than youth. Do not waste a single day. Challenge yourselves! Grow! There is no time to be idle.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

“Young people, be philosophers, be thinkers, be global citizens. The future of humanity depends on the inner strength of the young.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“In youth, you should aim for the highest goals. Dream big. And then do everything in your power to make those dreams a reality.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Youth and the Writings of Nichiren Daishonin

“The voice of a single young person, if it rings with truth and conviction, can change the world.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 11

“Youth should be rebellious. They should challenge the status quo, question authority, and refuse to accept injustice.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“Be a person of substance, not merely of surface. The depth of your character will determine your influence in the world.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

“The struggles you go through in your youth will become the foundation for a life of greatness. Do not avoid difficulty — embrace it.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Youth and the Writings of Nichiren Daishonin

“I place my greatest trust in young people. It is the young who will shape the future. Everything depends on you.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 26

“Study hard. Read voraciously. The person who has stopped learning has stopped growing. Growth is life.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“Each of you possesses a unique mission that no one else can fulfill. The question is whether you will discover it and dedicate your life to it.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The Wisdom for Creating Happiness and Peace

“Do not compare yourself to others. You are you. Your path is your path. Walk it with confidence.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

“The person who works the hardest in their youth will enjoy the greatest freedom and happiness in their later years.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“Have the courage to be yourself. There is no one in the whole universe exactly like you. That is your strength.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Youth and the Writings of Nichiren Daishonin

“The future belongs to those who have the courage to dream and the determination to make those dreams come true.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 20
