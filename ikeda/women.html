Daisaku Ikeda on Women

Ikeda Sensei's deep respect for women and his vision of the 21st century as the century of women — where women's leadership transforms society.

15 quotes

“The 21st century must be the century of women. The happiness of women is the happiness of all humanity.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 26

“Women who practice Buddhism are the sun. They illuminate the lives of everyone around them with warmth and light.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The Wisdom for Creating Happiness and Peace

“A society that does not value women, that does not protect women's rights, that does not listen to women's voices — such a society has no future.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Peace Proposal to the United Nations

“The strength of women is not physical strength. It is the strength of heart, the strength of compassion, the strength of perseverance through any hardship.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

“Never look down on yourself. You are a precious child of the Buddha. You have within you the power to change the world.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“Mothers are the greatest unsung heroes of society. The love and dedication of a mother shapes the future of humanity.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The Wisdom for Creating Happiness and Peace

“Women who stand up with courage and compassion can move mountains. Never underestimate the power of a determined woman.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 18

“The Lotus Sutra teaches the enlightenment of women — a revolutionary doctrine that affirmed women's equality at a time when no other teaching did so.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The Wisdom for Creating Happiness and Peace

“A woman's smile has the power to dispel darkness. A woman's determination has the power to change society.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

“Do not let anyone tell you that you cannot achieve your dreams because you are a woman. Break through every barrier. Show the world what you are capable of.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

“When women are empowered, families thrive, communities prosper, and nations flourish.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, Peace Proposal to the United Nations

“The future will be built by women who refuse to accept the status quo. Women of vision, women of courage, women of compassion.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 20

“A society in which women can shine is a society in which all people can shine. The empowerment of women is the empowerment of all.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The Wisdom for Creating Happiness and Peace

“Women, be strong. Be proud. Be confident in your own worth. You are infinitely precious.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

“Nichiren Buddhism teaches absolute equality. There is no distinction between men and women in the eyes of the Mystic Law.”

— Daisaku Ikeda, The Wisdom for Creating Happiness and Peace
