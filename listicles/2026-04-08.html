When Everyone Else Stays Silent: 5 Quotes on Finding Your Voice

April 08, 2026 · Courage · Ikeda Sensei’s Guidance
1
“Courage is not the absence of fear. Courage is feeling fear, recognizing fear, and still taking action.”
— Daisaku Ikeda, Discussions on Youth

True bravery means moving forward despite the butterflies in your stomach, not waiting until they disappear.

2
“A great human revolution in just a single individual will help achieve a change in the destiny of a nation and, further, will enable a change in the destiny of all humankind.”
— Daisaku Ikeda, The Human Revolution

Your personal transformation isn't selfish—it creates ripple effects that can change the world around you.

3
“No matter what happens, I will not be defeated. I will not run away. I will not complain. I will not be negative. I will keep advancing.”
— Daisaku Ikeda, The New Human Revolution, Vol. 1

When life knocks you down, this becomes your daily mantra for getting back up stronger.

4
“Be the kind of person who, when your feet hit the floor in the morning, makes the devil say, 'Oh no, they're up!'”
— Daisaku Ikeda, Youth and the Writings of Nichiren Daishonin

Start each day with such fierce determination that even your problems know they're in trouble.

5
“The true hero is one who conquers his own anger and hatred.”
— Daisaku Ikeda, For Today and Tomorrow

Real strength isn't overpowering others—it's mastering the negative emotions that try to control you.
